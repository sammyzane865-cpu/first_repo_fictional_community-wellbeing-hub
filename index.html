About Us

We've Been Here Since 2015

The Community Wellbeing Hub was founded with a single belief — that everyone deserves access to quality mental health, wellness, and community support, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Over the past eleven years, we've grown from a small counselling service into a full-spectrum wellbeing centre, offering everything from individual therapy and group sessions to fitness programs, nutrition coaching, life skills workshops, and a 24/7 crisis helpline. We're proud to serve thousands of people in our community each year.

Our team of licensed therapists, wellness coaches, dietitians, and community volunteers share one goal: helping you thrive. Whether you're navigating a difficult moment or simply looking to invest in your wellbeing, we're here for you every step of the way.
